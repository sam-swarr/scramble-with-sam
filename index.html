Scramble Solver

Automatically tab after typing a letter

About:


In Zynga's Scramble with Friends, players are given two minutes to find as many words as possible by stringing together adjacent letters from a 4x4 grid. As an avid fan of the game, I thought it would be interesting to figure out an efficient way to algorithmically find all possible words for a given board. The result is this solver!


To use:


  • 1. Enter the letters by clicking on the squares above and typing.
  • 2. Add any letter or word multipliers by clicking on the appropriate square and hitting SPACE. Hitting SPACE will cycle through the multipliers in the following order:
    • a. Double Letter
    • b. Double Word
    • c. Triple Letter
    • d. Triple Word
    • e. None
  • 3. Hit ENTER or push the Solve button to solve!.
  • 4. Click on entries in the results table to see detailed information!
