Controls

  • Left and Right Arrows - Paddle Movement
  • Ctrl - Launch Ball
  • P - Pause
  • R - Restart
  • 1 and 2 - Jump to Level

Object

Deflect the ball with your paddle in order to wreck all the rectangles! Be careful, you only have 3 lives. Different bricks are worth different amounts and will slightly increase or decrease the ball's speed by a different amount. Some bricks drop powerups (sticky-paddle and long-paddle)!
